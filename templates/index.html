Frequently Asked Questions

What ad formats are supported? ▼
We support all standard IAB ad sizes including 728x90 (Leaderboard), 300x250 (Medium Rectangle), 468x60 (Banner), 160x600 (Skyscraper), 160x300 (Wide Skyscraper), and 320x50 (Mobile Banner). More formats coming soon!
Is this really free? ▼
Yes! This tool is 100% free and will remain free forever. It's open source, so you can even host it yourself if you prefer. No hidden fees, no premium tiers, no credit card required.
How do I embed ads on my website? ▼
Simply copy the embed code snippet (like <script src="..."></script>) and paste it into your HTML where you want the ad to appear. The ad will automatically load and rotate. See our Publisher Guide for detailed instructions.
Do I need to sign up or create an account? ▼
Nope! You can preview ads and get embed codes instantly without any registration. Just browse, test, and embed.
Can I use this for commercial projects? ▼
Absolutely! This project is open source and free to use for both personal and commercial purposes. Check the LICENSE file for more details.
How is my privacy protected? ▼
We don't track users, set cookies, or collect personal data. We believe in privacy-first advertising. Your browsing activity remains private.
