HTML5 Banner Validator – Pre-Flight Checklist

Use this quick checklist to validate your HTML5 banners before you upload them to any ad server or network.

1. Basic technical checks

  • Total ZIP size is within your network’s limit (often 150–300 KB).
  • Banner exports as a clean HTML5 package (no external fonts that break).
  • No console errors when you open the banner in Chrome DevTools.

2. Click & tracking

  • Exactly one clickable area (no overlapping clickable elements).
  • Click opens the landing page in a new tab.
  • No blocked pop-ups, no auto-downloads, no redirects to suspicious URLs.

3. Visual behaviour

  • Animations end after a reasonable time (usually under 30 seconds).
  • No flashing or strobing that could be considered harmful.
  • Nothing “jumps” outside the banner size (no overflow / scrollbars).
